About SelfCraft

SelfCraft is a unique platform where you can upload your own design ideas and have them produced by skilled manufacturers at a fair price. Whether you're looking to create custom furniture, unique decor, or other handmade designs, we make it possible. You control the price, and our network of talented manufacturers is ready to turn your ideas into reality.

Founded in 2024 by a passionate team of students, SelfCraft was created with the mission to empower creators and connect them with skilled craftsmen and manufacturers who can bring their visions to life. It's simple – upload your design, set a price, and connect with the right people to make it happen.

How It Works

  • Step 1: Upload Your Design – Share your custom design by uploading it directly to the SelfCraft platform. Add all the necessary details, such as dimensions, materials, and style.
  • Step 2: Set Your Price – Decide on the price you'd like to offer for the production of your design. You have full control over the cost!
  • Step 3: Connect with Manufacturers – Manufacturers who are interested in your design will review your submission and provide quotes, or reach out to you for more details.
  • Step 4: Make It Happen – Once you agree on the price and details, the manufacturer will begin producing your design and ship it to you when it's ready!
